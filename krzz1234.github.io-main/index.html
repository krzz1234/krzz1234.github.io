Fuzzy Controller for the Inverted Pendulum Problem - (C++)

The objective of this project was to develop a fuzzy controller capable of stabilizing an inverted pendulum on a cart. The controller was designed to enable the artificial intelligence to calculate the position and velocity of the cart, as well as the angle and angular velocity of the pole, and utilize this data to maintain balance of the pendulum.
